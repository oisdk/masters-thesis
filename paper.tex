\begin{abstract}
  Finiteness in constructive mathematics is more complex than its classical
  counterpart.
  This paper will explore five notions of finiteness, and the relations between
  them, in Cubical Agda.
  We will show that Kuratowski finiteness forms a $\Pi$-pretopos, and then we
  will use our finiteness predicates to construct a proof search library for
  predicates with finite domains.
  We will use this library to produce a solver for the countdown problem.
\end{abstract}
